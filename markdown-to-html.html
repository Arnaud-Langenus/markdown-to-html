A funny story

When I was in Zimbabwe I went rafting on the Zambezi river. There is a rapid (a sudden increase in water velocity and turbulence)
called The Washing Machine , where the raft capsized, and everyone on board got thrown into the coarsing river. Everyone made it
back on board apart from, of course, me. A life guard came to save me with his hilariously short 1m long kayak, where I had to grab
on like a baby monkey grabs on to the back of his mother. This did not go to plan, because the next great rapid called The Terminator,
a humongous torrent the size of the pool of tears I will cry once JavaScript starts, swallowed me whole, and dragged me down to the
bottom of the river, where I stayed for exactly 11 seconds. The only proof I have of this adventure is a picture of me on the moment
of capsizing, and when my lifeguard decided I wasn't wet enough already (Proof.).

TL;DR

I learned that wildwater rafting is not fun if you can't breathe underwater.
